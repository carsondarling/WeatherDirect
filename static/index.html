Weather Direct

Getting the weather should be easy. Weather.com doesn't make it so. Now, get right to the weather.

weather.carsondarling.com/

The idea originally came about from a friend of mine, Trevor Filter, who pointed out that Weather.com should have simple URL redirection. Ever since he pointed it out, it's bugged me. Yes, I know that you can simply Google "weather" and it will get you close, but that's just not as fulfilling.

To make this work, the search string you enter is run through Google's geocoding API, and either a postal code or a city & state is pulled out. If nothing is found, it just uses Weather.com's search on the string you provided. This gives slightly better results that just directly searching.
